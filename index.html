Deployable Remote Online Network Evaluator (D.R.O.N.E)

Currently, the West Virginia Office of Technology (WVOT) cyber security department is under manned and has minimal resources to provide support to all the networks and systems not just in the county, but the entire state. There are fifty-five counties in the state that span over 24,000 square miles. These networks and systems experience both internal and external threats year-round. They also must worry about criminal actions and illegal access to these machines. Due to the skills required and time needed to monitor and troubleshoot all the networks, a backlog of issues can occur. The issue mainly arises due to traveling costs and time for traveling to these areas repeatedly. They have asked for a solution to provide a way for their department monitor and fix these issue from a central work station.
